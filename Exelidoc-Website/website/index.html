Local AI vs Cloud AI

🖥️ Local AI (Ollama)

  • Runs entirely on your machine
  • Complete privacy - no data sent anywhere
  • Works offline - no internet needed
  • No API costs - it's free
  • Slightly slower (depends on your computer)
  • Good for sensitive documents

☁️ Cloud AI (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini)

  • Access to the most powerful AI models
  • Very fast responses
  • Pay-per-token (affordable)
  • Requires internet connection
  • Data sent to provider servers
  • Best for power users
