Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We don't collect, store, or track your data.

🔒 Quick Summary

This website does not collect, store, or track any personal information. We don't use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. Your privacy is fully protected.

Website Privacy

What We Don't Do

  • No tracking: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tools
  • No cookies: We don't set any cookies on your browser
  • No data collection: We don't collect your IP address, location, or browsing behavior
  • No email lists: We don't have mailing lists or newsletter signups
  • No ads: We don't serve advertisements or third-party content

What Information We Receive

When you visit our website, your web browser may automatically send certain information to our hosting provider (Netlify, GitHub Pages, etc.), such as:

  • Your IP address
  • The page you visited
  • The time you visited
  • Your browser type

This information is logged by the hosting provider for server maintenance and security purposes only. We do not access or use this data for any other purpose.

Third-Party Hosting

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages or Netlify, which are secure, industry-standard hosting platforms. You can review their privacy policies:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Netlify Privacy Policy

Exelidoc Application Privacy

Local Data Storage

When you use the Exelidoc application on your computer, it stores data locally on your machine:

  • Token usage: We track how many tokens you've used (stored locally)
  • User account: Your user ID and subscription tier (stored locally)
  • Configuration: Your API keys and preferences (encrypted, stored locally)

This data never leaves your computer. It's stored in a SQLite database at `~/.exelidoc/`

Cloud AI vs Local AI

If you use Cloud AI (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini):

  • Your prompts are sent to the AI provider's servers
  • Your API key is sent securely with each request
  • Review the privacy policies of your chosen provider:
    • OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • Anthropic Privacy Policy
    • Google Privacy Policy

If you use Local AI (Ollama):

  • All AI processing happens on your computer
  • No data is sent anywhere
  • Complete privacy and control

What We Never Do With Your Data

  • ❌ We never sell your data
  • ❌ We never share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We never use your data for marketing
  • ❌ We never track you across the web
  • ❌ We never build profiles about you

Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Encrypted storage: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
  • Secure transmission: Cloud AI requests use HTTPS encryption
  • No server tracking: We don't run servers that collect your data
  • Open source approach: Our application code is transparent and auditable

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date below.
